rEMOTION Play Festival.

rEMTION play festival was a socially-distanced open-admission play festival performed over Zoom and streamed live over Twitch. I directed three shows, one written for the stage and adapted to the medium, and two written specifically for the live-stream medium.

rEMOTION premiered in June, 2020.
